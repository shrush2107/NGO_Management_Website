Some Frequently Asked Questions you might want to know about...

Can we make monetary donations?

We do not provide and option to make or take monetary donations because of safety reasons. We are a new website at the moment and wouldn't want anyone to suffer loss or face diappoinment.

Is it very complicated to donate online?

Not at all. It's simple and safe. Here's what you have to do to make a donation online. Choose the donation you want to make from the requirments list. Your selection will be displayed to the NGO. Please check the information carefully and make sure that it is correct. Once you click on donate, we'll notify the respective NGO about your interest. They'll contact you back and provide a pickup. You will get an immediate confirmation of your donation, receive an email confirmation message.

How do I sign up if I am a NGO?

vFor NGOs, we do not provide an option for direct sign up due to authenticity reasons. So to register, you’ll have to fill up the form available here Register Here. Then, we’ll take 3-4 working days to verify the information. If approved, we’ll be personally mailing you yourlogin cerdentials.

How do I function as an NGO once registration is complete?

NGOs can post and delete their requirements to make it visible for the donors. You’ll see a “POSTS” tab on the top. Once you click on that, you will the requirements you have already posted and will also get an option to delete them. By clicking on delete button on their right, you can delete them. You can also post more by clicking on “post”. You’ll see it lsied once you refresh the page.

As a donor, how can I donate?

We do not provide and option to make or take monetary donations because of safety reasons.But you can donate stuff like clothes,food,hygiene essentials, mediciens,etc. Just click on "Donate" and we will email the ngo that you are interested and then can can contact you.
